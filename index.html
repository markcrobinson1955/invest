03.Upload your portfolio

When the analyzer asks, upload your portfolio in whatever format you have — CSV, Excel, PDF, a screenshot, or plain text. If you use tax-advantaged accounts and want tax-location advice, include account names (IRA, Roth, Taxable).

Your portfolio goes only to the AI assistant you chose. This page never sees or stores it.

What you'll receive

A full alignment report that includes:

  • Preliminary assessments — correlation regime, liquidity tiers, currency exposure, concentration checks
  • Scoring against each of the five frameworks, with red and green flags identified
  • A prioritized action list ranked by cross-framework impact
  • Drawdown stress tests against historical scenarios
  • Tax location recommendations, if you are a US taxpayer and included account names

This is educational analysis, not personalized financial advice. The analyzer never recommends specific securities — only allocation categories and portfolio construction principles.
